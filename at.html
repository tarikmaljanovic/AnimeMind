Attck On Titan
進撃の巨人

Opinion:

Attack on Titan is a Shounen action anime with a very unique plot and look to it.
The anime takes place in a made up world where human kind had to flee behind the 3 walls,
Maria, Rose and Sina, to survive from the titans that are terrorising them every
moment. The main characters, Eren, Mikasa and Armin, along with the supporting
characters have a very unique backstory and personality. The anime does not have any fillers
and every single episode captures the viewers attention and is crucial to understanding the
the some what complx story. Animation and music make it a lot more enjoyable and likable.
On my personal list it is ranked number 4.
